Qwen3 unifies reasoning and fast response modes across dense and MoE LLMs.

The report presents a model family spanning 0.6B to 235B parameters, with thinking/non-thinking mode control, a thinking budget, multilingual expansion, and Apache-2.0 public model access.

0.6B-235B

Dense and Mixture-of-Expert model scales.

2 modes

Thinking for hard reasoning, non-thinking for faster context responses.

Expanded from Qwen2.5's reported 29 languages and dialects.

Apache 2.0

Models are publicly accessible under Apache 2.0 per arXiv abstract.
